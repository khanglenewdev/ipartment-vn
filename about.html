Brand Story & German Standard

Fifteen years of
knowing what home means.

ipartment was founded in Germany with a simple but powerful idea: business travel and relocation don't have to feel like living out of a suitcase. We built apartments that feel like home — thoughtfully designed, fully equipped, and service-oriented from day one.

Over 15 years, that idea grew into 1,200+ apartments across Germany and Switzerland. Now, we bring that same DNA to Vietnam. ipartment Thao Dien is not a copy-paste — it's a careful translation of German precision into Vietnamese warmth, built specifically for the expat and professional community of Ho Chi Minh City.

Location & Map

Thao Dien — where
Saigon slows down, beautifully.

Thao Dien is District 2's most sought-after expat enclave — known for its tree-lined streets, international dining scene, boutique cafés, and riverfront lifestyle. It's Saigon at its most liveable.

ipartment is located on Xuan Thuy Street — a quiet, walkable address that puts you 15 minutes from the buzz of District 1, steps from Metro Line 1, and surrounded by international schools (BVIS, BIS, ISHCMC) and co-working spaces.

🏙️District 1 — 15 minutes by car or Grab
✈️Tan Son Nhat Airport — 25 minutes
🏫International schools — BVIS, BIS, ISHCMC all nearby
