Brand Story, German Standard

Fifteen years of
knowing what home means.

ipartment was founded in Germany with a simple but powerful idea. Business travel and relocation should not feel like living out of a suitcase. We built apartments that feel like home, thoughtfully designed, fully equipped, and service-oriented from day one.

Over 15 years that idea grew into 1,200+ apartments across Germany and Switzerland. Now we bring that same DNA to Vietnam. ipartment Thao Dien is not a copy-paste. It is a careful translation of German precision into Vietnamese warmth, built for the expat and professional community of Ho Chi Minh City.

Our Journey

From Cologne to Saigon,
fifteen years in five chapters.

2011

Founded in Cologne, Germany

ipartment opens its first 28 units in Cologne, designed for visiting consultants and pharma execs tired of bland hotel chains. The "apartment that thinks like a hotel" idea is born.

2015

Expansion across Germany

300+ apartments live across Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich and Hamburg. The corporate B2B programme launches, signing global enterprise contracts.

2019

Crossing into Switzerland

First Swiss locations open in Zurich and Basel. Applying the German playbook with Swiss precision. 1,000+ apartments mark.

2024

Plans for Asia announced

After two years of research, Saigon is selected as the first Southeast Asia market. Chosen for its expat density, growing tech sector and Thao Dien sweet spot.

2026

ipartment Vietnam opens

We open our doors on Xuan Thuy Street, Thao Dien. The same standards, translated for Saigon. This is just the beginning.
