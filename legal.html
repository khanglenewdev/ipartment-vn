Terms and Conditions

Effective: 1 May 2026

Welcome to ipartment Vietnam. By booking a stay, browsing this website, or simply breathing in the general vicinity of our apartments, you agree to the following terms.

Note: These terms apply to all guests, including your cousin who "just needs a place for two nights" and definitely will not cook instant noodles at 2am and leave the pot in the sink.

1. Definitions

"ipartment", "we", "us", or "the management" refers to ipartment Vietnam. "Guest" or "you" refers to any individual who has made or is considering making a booking. "The apartment" refers to the specific unit reserved under your booking confirmation.

2. Bookings and Reservations

All bookings are subject to availability and are confirmed only upon receipt of a booking confirmation from ipartment. A reservation is not guaranteed until full or partial payment has been processed and a written confirmation has been issued to the email address provided at the time of booking.

ipartment reserves the right to decline any reservation at its sole discretion, including but not limited to reservations made under suspicious circumstances, fictitious names, or an unusual number of air mattresses.

3. Check-In and Check-Out

Standard check-in time is 14:00 (2:00 PM) ICT. Standard check-out time is 12:00 (noon) ICT. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Guests who have not vacated the premises by the agreed check-out time may be gently nudged, and then firmly nudged.

Clause 3.1 (The Noodle Rule): Guests are welcome to cook in the kitchen at any hour. However abandoning unwashed cookware in the sink overnight constitutes a breach of the sacred covenant between ipartment and its guests, and will be documented in your guest profile with a small but meaningful frown.

4. Payment and Rates

All rates are quoted in Vietnamese Dong (VND) and include applicable service charges. Rates are subject to change without prior notice for new bookings. Your confirmed rate will not change once a booking is finalised. Discounted weekly (18% off) and monthly (38% off) rates apply automatically based on your length of stay.

Payment methods accepted include bank transfer, cash on arrival (for short stays), and major credit cards. ipartment does not accept payment in Dogecoin, loyalty points from unrelated airlines, or promises of a positive Google review in lieu of deposit.

5. Cancellations and Modifications

Cancellations made more than 7 days before the check-in date are eligible for a full refund minus any applicable processing fees. Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in are non-refundable. No-shows are treated as full cancellations and will be charged at the full booking value.

Modifications to booking dates are subject to availability and rate differences. ipartment will make reasonable efforts to accommodate changes but cannot guarantee the same apartment unit or rate tier for modified stays.

6. House Rules

Guests agree to:

  • Treat the apartment and its contents with reasonable care.
  • Keep noise to a considerate level between 22:00 and 08:00. Thao Dien is a neighbourhood, not a festival.
  • Not smoke inside the apartment or on interior balconies.
  • Not host events or parties that exceed the stated maximum occupancy.
  • Report any damage or maintenance issues immediately rather than strategically placing a plant in front of them.
Clause 6.5 (The Durian Clause): Durian is a beloved fruit of Southeast Asia and we respect that deeply. However durian is strictly prohibited inside the apartment at all times. Violators will be asked to leave, not because we judge their taste, but because the smell has once outlasted a three-month lease.

7. Occupancy

Each apartment type has a maximum occupancy limit: Compact Studio XS (4 guests), Studio S (4 guests), Apartment M (6 guests), Long-Stay Suite L (8 guests). Exceeding maximum occupancy without prior written approval constitutes a breach of these terms.

8. Liability

ipartment is not liable for loss, theft, or damage to guests' personal belongings unless caused directly by the negligence of ipartment staff. Guests are strongly encouraged to obtain appropriate travel or renters' insurance for the duration of their stay.

9. Early Termination

If a guest wishes to vacate before their confirmed checkout date, they must provide written notice at least 48 hours in advance. Refunds for unused nights are at the discretion of ipartment and depend on the original rate tier and booking terms applied.

10. Governing Law

These Terms and Conditions are governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Ho Chi Minh City.

Final note: We wrote these terms because a lawyer told us we had to. But we also run a pretty relaxed ship. If something goes wrong, please just talk to us first. We genuinely want to sort it out over a coffee in the lobby, not in a courtroom.

Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 May 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data ipartment Vietnam collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. We will not sell your data. We will not share it with sketchy third parties.

1. Data We Collect

When you make a booking or create an account, we collect:

  • Your full name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Booking details including dates, apartment type, and number of guests
  • Payment information (processed securely. We do not store full card details)
  • Government-issued ID information as required by Vietnamese law
  • Communication records if you contact us via email or messaging

When you visit our website, we may also collect standard web analytics data such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

What we do NOT collect: Your deepest fears. Your opinion of your ex. Your Spotify listening history. ipartment is a hospitality brand, not a surveillance operation.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and manage your booking
  • Communicate with you about your stay
  • Comply with Vietnamese legal requirements
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Send you occasional promotional communications. Only if you have opted in.

3. Data Sharing

ipartment does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as payment processors) solely to operate our business, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on servers located within or accessible from Vietnam. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

Clause 4.1 (The Password Note): If your password is "123456" or "ipartment", we will not lecture you. But we will silently judge you, and we strongly encourage you to change it.

5. Cookies

This website uses cookies to maintain session state (such as your booking progress) and to collect anonymous analytics data. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or serve targeted advertising.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data held by ipartment. To exercise these rights, please contact us at khangle.forwork@gmail.com. We will respond within 14 business days.

7. Retention

Guest records are typically retained for a minimum of 3 years following the end of a stay, as required by Vietnamese hospitality regulations.

8. Children

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of the document. If changes are significant, we will notify active guests by email.
