Booking and Pricing

You can book directly through our website by selecting your preferred apartment category, check-in and check-out dates, and completing your details. For long-stay or corporate bookings, we recommend emailing us at khangle.forwork@gmail.com for a personalised quote.

All ipartment rates are all-inclusive. Your rate covers 500 Mbps fibre Wi-Fi, housekeeping (frequency varies by category), all utilities (electricity, water, air conditioning), and access to shared amenities. There are no hidden fees or add-ons.

We accept stays from 1 night. However our best rates are available for stays of 7+ nights (18% off), and exclusive corporate rates apply from 28 nights (up to 38% off). The longer you stay the more you save.

Yes. For corporate clients and groups booking multiple apartments, we offer exclusive monthly pricing, centralized billing, and a dedicated account manager. Contact khangle.forwork@gmail.com to discuss your requirements.

For stays under 7 nights: free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. For stays of 7 to 27 nights: free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. For stays of 28+ nights: please refer to your corporate agreement or contact us directly.

Payment is not collected when you submit your booking request. Our team will contact you within 2 hours to confirm availability and arrange payment via bank transfer, credit card, or cash on arrival (for short stays).

Yes. For stays of 28+ nights we offer corporate rates of up to 38% off, billed monthly with utilities, internet, and housekeeping all included. Tell us your dates and we will send an exact monthly quote.

For short stays there is usually no deposit. For long stays and stays with a pet we may ask for a refundable deposit, which we always explain clearly before you commit.

Yes. We can issue an invoice or VAT receipt with the details your company needs for an expense claim, including a company VAT invoice. Send your billing details when you book or email khangle.forwork@gmail.com.

Rates can vary around peak periods such as Tet, so the surest way to know is to share your exact dates. We always show the clear all-inclusive price up front, with no surprises.

Yes. Your rate covers Wi-Fi, housekeeping, and all utilities, including running the air conditioning as much as you like. You never watch the meter or get a surprise bill.

The Apartments

XS (25-30 m²) is our compact studio, ideal for short stays. S (35-40 m²) adds a seating and dining area. M (45-55 m²) is a spacious apartment with designated living, dining, and home office zones. L (65-75 m²) is our largest, with a separate bedroom, large living room, and family-sized kitchen, built for long-term living.

Yes. All apartments include a fully functional kitchen with hob, refrigerator, microwave, kettle, toaster, and cooking essentials. Category M and L include a full oven and dishwasher. We provide starter packs of basic cooking items on arrival.

Yes. Housekeeping is included in all rates. XS: weekly. S: bi-weekly. M: weekly with linen change. L: twice-weekly with priority service. Additional housekeeping can be arranged on request.

All apartments come with high-speed fibre Wi-Fi at minimum 300 Mbps (categories M and L: 500 Mbps). Every apartment has a dedicated router. No shared network, no random throttling. Tested across video calls, large uploads, and gaming sessions.

Small pets (under 15 kg) are allowed in M and L apartments with prior notice and a pet deposit. Please contact us before booking to confirm. We adore animals but need to plan for them.

Our M and L apartments include a full oven and dishwasher, while the compact XS and S kitchens use a cooktop, microwave, and fridge, which is normal for the city. If you would like an air fryer or countertop oven in a smaller unit, just ask and we will arrange it.

Yes. Every apartment has a washing machine, plus a drying rack or balcony to air dry, which suits the climate here. Dryers are less common, so tell us if you need one and we will confirm which apartments have it.

Yes. Apartments are set up for remote workers, with a desk, a comfortable chair, plenty of power outlets, and fast Wi-Fi on a dedicated router. Vietnam runs on 220V with Type A and C sockets, so most chargers fit straight in.

Yes. The M and L apartments suit families, and we can arrange baby gear such as a cot, high chair, or stroller delivered clean and ready to your apartment through trusted local rental services. Tell us your needs when you book.

Yes. Every apartment has air conditioning, and the electricity to run it is included in your all-inclusive rate, so you stay cool without ever watching the meter.

Apartments have a TV, and many are smart TVs you can sign in to for your own streaming, with the fast Wi-Fi handling it easily. Tell us your apartment and we will confirm.

Check-in and Check-out

Standard check-in is from 15:00. Check-out is by 11:00. We offer flexible check-in 24/7 via digital self check-in. You will receive your access code before arrival. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged subject to availability.

No. ipartment uses a fully digital self check-in system. You will receive all access details via email and SMS before your arrival. If you prefer a personal welcome, just let us know and our team will be happy to meet you.

Vietnamese law requires us to register all guests with the local police. Please upload a photo of your passport (and visa if applicable) via the link we send before arrival. It takes 30 seconds and avoids any check-in delay.

Yes. Digital self check-in works 24/7, so a midnight or early-morning arrival is no problem. Your access code is ready before you land, so you can go straight in even off a red-eye flight.

Yes. If you arrive before check-in or need to leave bags after check-out, we can usually arrange luggage drop or early access so you are not stuck waiting. Just tell us your arrival time.

Late check-out can be arranged subject to availability. If no one is arriving right after you, we are usually happy to give you extra time, which is handy before an evening flight. Just ask the day before.

No. It is handled online. You upload a photo of your passport, and visa if applicable, via a secure link before arrival, and we complete the required local registration for you. It takes about 30 seconds on your side.

Location

We are located on Xuan Thuy Street, Thao Dien, District 2 (Thu Duc City), Ho Chi Minh City. The building is a 3-minute walk from Thao Dien Metro Station (Line 1), 15 minutes from District 1 by car, and 25 minutes from Tan Son Nhat Airport.

Yes. Secure underground parking is available for guests with motorbikes and cars. Parking is included in all rates for motorbikes. Car parking is available at a small additional daily or monthly rate.

Yes. Airport pickup from Tan Son Nhat International is available as an optional add-on at checkout (450,000 VND one-way). Your driver will meet you at arrivals with a name card.

Yes. Thao Dien is one of HCMC's best food districts. Within 5 minutes walking you will find international restaurants, riverside cafes, brunch spots, vegan options, and authentic Vietnamese street food. See our Thao Dien guide for curated lists.

Easily. Thao Dien Metro Station (Line 1) is a 3-minute walk and reaches District 1 in minutes, Grab cars and bikes are everywhere, and we can arrange a private driver or a motorbike or bicycle rental. District 1 is about 15 minutes away by car.

Yes. Thao Dien is the city's hub for pet care, with several English-speaking vet clinics, pet shops, and pet-friendly cafes a short distance away. Tell us if you are bringing a pet and we will point you to the closest options.

Yes. Thao Dien is well known for its international schools, which is a big reason relocating families choose the area. If you are moving with children, tell us and we can point you to the nearest options.

Thao Dien is known for Western-style supermarkets and gourmet shops with imported groceries, all within a short walk or ride. Ask us and we will point you to the closest one for your shopping list.

Yes. Thao Dien and the surrounding area have international-standard clinics with English-speaking doctors close by, and full international hospitals are a short drive away. If you have a medical need during your stay, ask us and we will point you to the right one.
