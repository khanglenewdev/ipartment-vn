Booking & Pricing

You can book directly through our website by selecting your preferred apartment category, check-in/check-out dates, and completing your details in My Account. For long-stay or corporate bookings, we recommend emailing us at hello@ipartment.vn for a personalised quote.

All ipartment rates are all-inclusive. Your rate covers high-speed Wi-Fi, housekeeping (frequency varies by category), all utilities (electricity, water, air conditioning), and access to shared amenities. There are no hidden fees or add-ons.

We accept stays from 1 night. However, our best rates are available for stays of 7+ nights, and exclusive corporate rates apply from 28 nights. The longer you stay, the more you save.

Yes. For corporate clients and groups booking multiple apartments, we offer exclusive monthly pricing, centralized billing, and a dedicated account manager. Contact corporate@ipartment.vn to discuss your requirements.

For stays under 7 nights: free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. For stays of 7–27 nights: free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. For stays of 28+ nights: please refer to your corporate agreement or contact us directly for terms.

The Apartments

XS (25–30 m²) is our compact studio, ideal for short stays. S (35–40 m²) adds a seating and dining area — great for mid-term stays. M (45–55 m²) is a spacious apartment with designated living, dining, and home office zones. L (65–75 m²) is our largest, with a separate bedroom, large living room, and family-sized kitchen — built for long-term living.

Yes. All apartments include a fully functional kitchen with hob, refrigerator, microwave, kettle, toaster, and cooking essentials. Category M and L include a full oven and dishwasher. We provide starter packs of basic cooking items on arrival.

Yes. Housekeeping is included in all rates. XS: weekly. S: bi-weekly. M: weekly with linen change. L: twice-weekly with priority service. Additional housekeeping can be arranged on request.

Check-in & Check-out

Standard check-in is from 15:00. Check-out is by 11:00. We offer flexible check-in 24/7 via digital self check-in — you'll receive your access code before arrival. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged subject to availability.

No. ipartment uses a fully digital self check-in system. You'll receive all access details via email and SMS before your arrival. If you prefer a personal welcome, just let us know and our team will be happy to meet you.

Location

We are located on Xuan Thuy Street, Thao Dien, District 2 (Thu Duc City), Ho Chi Minh City. The building is a 3-minute walk from Thao Dien Metro Station (Line 1), 15 minutes from District 1 by car, and 25 minutes from Tan Son Nhat Airport.

Yes. Secure underground parking is available for guests with motorbikes and cars. Parking is included in all rates for motorbikes. Car parking is available at a small additional daily or monthly rate.
